Pros

  • Same-day print at FedEx and UPS
  • Squarer body: each column a bit taller
  • Slightly more vertical for the hero plot
  • Travel risk is lower (reprint on arrival)

Cons

  • ~12% less total surface area
  • Body cols are narrower at the same height
  • Title may wrap into two lines
  • Less "Neutrino-conference standard"

Hidden question: is print logistics on the day-of (4×3 ft, same-day) more important than the extra ~12% of working area (A0)? If you have a buffer day in Hawaiʻi before the conference, A0 is the more standard pick.
