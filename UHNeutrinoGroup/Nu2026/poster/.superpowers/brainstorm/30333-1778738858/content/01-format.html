Which landscape format?

The conference allows either — they have different aspect ratios, which changes how the body columns fit.

A

A0 landscape — 1189 × 841 mm

Aspect 1.414 : 1 (√2, the ISO ratio). Taller body area; comfortably fits 4 narrow columns or 3 medium columns with a hero strip. UPS can print but requires one business day.

B

4 ft × 3 ft landscape — 1219 × 914 mm

Aspect 1.333 : 1 (4:3). Slightly squarer; 3 wide columns feel natural with a hero block on the left. Standard same-day stock at both FedEx and UPS — fewer logistics risks.
